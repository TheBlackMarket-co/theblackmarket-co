Intro

The Black Market is a gaming community over a decade in the making. Born from a TF2 trade server (possibly the oldest-running today), TBM is and will always be a group of old friends first. A healthy portion of us have known eachother for well over 10 years, from all corners of the map. While games may hype and die, we apparently do not. If you'd like to join this disfunctional family our Discord is a great place to start.
